Frequently Asked Auestions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you have the flexibility to cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle (monthly/annually), meaning you'll continue to have access to the service until that date. We do not charge cancellation fees. For detailed steps on how to cancel, please visit your "Account Settings" or contact our support team.

Do you offer refunds?

We do not offer prorated refunds for cancellations. As outlined in our cancellation policy, your service will remain active until the end of your current billing period, ensuring you receive the full value of your subscription. If you believe there's been a billing error, please contact our support team.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a permanent Free Plan rather than a time-limited free trial. Please refer to our Free Plan details for more information.

Can I get a custom plan if my needs don't fit the standard tiers?

Yes, we understand that every organization has distinct needs. If our standard tiers don't perfectly match your requirements, our Enterprise Plan offers custom pricing and features on demand. We'll work closely with you to build a plan that's precisely right for your enterprise or large project. Get in touch with us to plan your custom plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and popular digital wallets such as UPI, PayPal and Google Pay.
